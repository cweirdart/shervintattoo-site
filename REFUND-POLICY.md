# Refund Policy — Shervin Tattoo

**Draft — review with Shervin before publishing**

---

All sales of original artwork, limited-edition prints, and small-batch ceramics are final.

If your item arrives damaged in transit, please email **shervinx510@gmail.com** within **7 days of delivery** with your order number and photos of the damage. I'll work with you on a replacement (when available) or a full refund.

For apparel (shirts, etc.), unworn returns are accepted within **14 days of receipt** for store credit. Customer covers return shipping. Items must be returned in original condition with tags intact.

I'm a one-person operation, so please be patient with response times — I aim to reply within 3 business days.

---

## Things to decide with Shervin

- **Refund window:** 7 days for damage? 14 days? Match it to his actual capacity.
- **Apparel returns:** does he want to accept them at all? Many small artists make all sales final.
- **Restocking fee:** any percentage off for returns? (Most artists skip this.)
- **International shipping:** does he ship outside the US? If so, customs/duty disclaimers needed.
- **Lost packages:** what happens if USPS loses it? He could refund or replace, but worth setting expectation.
- **Custom orders:** if he ever does commissions through the shop, those should explicitly be "no refund" — make a separate clause.
